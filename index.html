Nick Fury---Director of S.H.I.E.L.D.

Nick Fury is a superhero under the American Marvel Comics. He first appeared in the first issue of "Sgt. Fury and His Howling Commandos" (May 1963). His full name is Nicholas Joseph Fury. He is a genetically modified warrior of the SSS program. He was a U.S. Army colonel during World War II. After the war, he became the director of SHIELD. As an intermediary between the U.S. government and the Avengers, he was also a secret Wall Keeper and Peeping people.

Nick Fury Jr. and the movie are as black as the Ultimate Universe. Nick Fury retired due to aging due to the failure of his serum. Nick Fury Jr. gradually became known as Nick Fury after the comics were serialized for a period of time.

Hawkeye---The best sniper in the world

Clint Barton, also known as Hawkeye, is a superhero under the American Marvel Comics. He first appeared in the 57th issue of "Suspense Stories" (September 1964) and was co-created by Stan Lee and Don Heck.

His full name is Clinton Francis Barton, who once went by the aliases of Goliath and Ronin. He was an orphan who grew up in the circus. He was taught by Swordsman and Shooter. He was extremely talented. He was awarded "Eagle Eye" and "World" when he was a child. "Best Sniper" because after seeing Iron Man saving people during a performance, he decided to use his abilities to become a superhero, and later joined the Avengers and became an important member of it.
